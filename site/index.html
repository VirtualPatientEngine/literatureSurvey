    Overview

    This page was last updated on 2024-03-26 09:32:14 UTC

    This website is a repository of curated collection of references, as well as, most recent and most popular publications on differentiable and learned simulator algorithms for developing digital twins with applications in drug discovery and development. Here we provide a list of resources with links to the associated manuscripts or websites.

    For further information about our group see our homepage.

    The Overview tab provides a summary of all sub-topics covered in the tabs, serving as a convenient starting point for seeking a general understanding or quick reference before delving into specific details within individual tabs. Please refer to the tabs Physics-informed GNNs, Neural ODEs, Koopman Theory, Latent Space Simulator, PINNs and Symbolic regression for detailed information.
